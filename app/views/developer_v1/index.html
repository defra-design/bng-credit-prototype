What is biodiversity net gain (BNG)?

Biodiversity net gain is an approach to development and land management, which aims to leave the natural environment in a measurably better state than it was before it was developed.

Biodiversity net gain will become a mandatory part of the Environment Act in November 2023, under the Town and Country Planning Act.

A minimum 10% biodiversity net gain

From November 2023, at least 10% biodiversity net gain is needed for most planning applications.

Natural England is working in partnership with the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra) to put this in place.
