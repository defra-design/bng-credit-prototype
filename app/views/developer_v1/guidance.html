If you're a developer

You'll need to show a minimum of 10% biodiversity net gain for your development projects.

We'll review the information you provide within 28 days. You can then submit it to your Local Planning Authority (LPA), to discharge your BNG planning condition.

Before you start

You'll need your Biodiversity Metric 3.1 calculation that was agreed with your Local Planning Authority when they approved your Gain Plan.

You'll also need the legal agreements and land boundary information for any off-site land you plan to use.
