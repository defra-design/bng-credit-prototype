Register biodiversity gain for developments

You'll need to show a minimum of 10% biodiversity net gain for your development projects.

We'll review the information you provide within 28 days. You can then submit it to your Local Planning Authority (LPA), to discharge your BNG planning condition.

Before you start

You'll need

  • the Biodiversity Metric 3.1 calculation which your Local Planning Authority approved for your draft Gain Plan
  • legal agreements for any off-site land you plan to use
  • land boundary details for your development site
