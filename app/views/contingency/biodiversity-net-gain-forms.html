Details

Use these forms to either:

  • authorise an agent to act on your behalf
  • register a biodiversity gain site
  • record off-site gain for a development project
  • buy statutory biodiversity credits

How to apply

  1. If you're an agent, the landowner, leaseholder or developer must complete the form 'authorise an agent to act on your behalf'. This must be sent with your application.
  2. Download and complete the forms you need.
  3. Create a new email and attach your completed forms.
  4. Send your email to bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk.
! Warning These forms must be completed on your computer using a PDF editor tool. We will not be accepting any handwritten application forms.

We will contact you within 28 days of receiving the forms to tell you what to do next.

When to pay

We will email you when we are ready for your payment.

After we receive your payment, we will let you know within 6 weeks if we:

  • accept or reject your application to record your development project
  • need more information
