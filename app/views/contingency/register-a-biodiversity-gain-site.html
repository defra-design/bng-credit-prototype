Register a biodiversity gain site

How to apply

You will need to download and complete forms:

  • Register a biodiversity gain site
  • Authorise an agent, if you're acting on behalf of someone else

Create a new email and attach your completed forms. Send your email to: bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk. We will not be accepting any handwritten application forms.

What happens next

We will email you back within 28 days with a 19-character reference number. You will need to reply to our email with your legal documentation so we can verify your gain site.

You must enter your reference number in the subject line every time you email us. This is so we can match your documents to your case file.

There could be a delay to your application if you do not give us the correct reference number.

How to pay

You will need to make a Bacs transfer of £658.70 within 28 days of us requesting the documents. We will send you our bank account details in an email.

Your payment could be lost if you do not use the correct reference number when you pay.

We will let you know within 6 weeks if we:

  • accept or reject your biodiversity gain site
  • need more information

How to register a biodiversity gain site and record a development project at the same time

  • Register a biodiversity gain site
  • Record off-site biodiversity gains for a development project
  • Authorise an agent, if you're acting on behalf of someone else

Send all forms in one email to bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk.

As well as everything you need to register a biodiversity gain site, you'll need the Biodiversity Metric 4.0 file for the development project. It must be the same file a local planning authority reviewed with the development project's Biodiversity Net Gain Statement.

You will not need to pay any more than £658.70.

Contact the Biodiversity Net Gain team if you need help

Telephone: 0190 050 8628

Monday to Friday: 8.30am to 5pm (except public holidays)
