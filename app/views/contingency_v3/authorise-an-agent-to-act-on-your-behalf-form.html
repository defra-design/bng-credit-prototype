Details

You can use an agent or representative to act on your behalf to either:

  • register a biodiversity gain site
  • record allocation of off-site biodiversity gains to a development

You can also use this form if you’re doing both tasks at the same time.

It must be the landowner or leaseholder who completes this form.

How to apply

We advise using Google Chrome to download and complete this form.
  1. Open and download the ‘Authorise an agent to act on your behalf’ form. Answer the questions on the form and save it.
  2. Send it to the agent or representative who will be acting on your behalf.
We will not accept any handwritten forms sent in by post.
