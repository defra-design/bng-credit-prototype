Details

This form is to register a biodiversity gain site and record allocation of off-site gains to a development at the same time.

Before you apply

If you're an agent, the landowner or leaseholder of the gain site you're registering must give consent. They will need to complete the 'Authorise an agent to act on your behalf' form. This must be sent with your application.

How to apply

We advise using Google Chrome to download and complete this form.
  1. Open and download the ‘Register a biodiversity gain site and record allocation of off-site gains to a development’ form. Answer the questions on the form and save it.
  2. Create a new email using your preferred email provider and attach your completed forms.
  3. In the subject line type 'Application to register a biodiversity gain site and record allocation of off-site gains to a development'.
  4. Send your email to bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk.
Do not send your documents until we ask for them. We will not accept any handwritten application forms sent in by post.

We will contact you within 28 days of receiving your application to tell you what to do next.

When to pay

We will email you when we are ready for your payment.

After we receive your payment, we will let you know within 6 weeks if we:

  • accept or reject your application to register a biodiversity gain site and record allocation of off-site gains to a development
  • need more information

Withdrawals and refunds

Once we have made a decision on your application you cannot withdraw it, or apply for a refund.

You may be able to get a refund if we have not made a decision within 6 weeks.
