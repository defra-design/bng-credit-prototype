Before you apply

You cannot use an agent or someone to act on your behalf to buy statutory biodiversity credits.

If you're buying as an individual, payment must be from your own personal bank account.

If you're buying as an organisation, payment must be from the bank account of the organisation.

A third-party supplier will use your information for customer due diligence checks. These are to check you are who you say you are and assess any risks for money laundering.

How to apply

We advise using Google Chrome to download and complete this form.
  1. Open and download the form. Complete the questions by typing your answers. Once you’re finished, save the form.
  2. Create a new email using your preferred email provider.
  3. Attach your completed form and your Biodiversity Metric. In the subject line type ‘Application to buy statutory biodiversity credits’.
  4. Send your email to bngcredits@naturalengland.org.uk.
We will not be accepting any handwritten application forms sent in by post.

We will contact you within 8 weeks of receiving your application to tell you if you’ve passed the checks.

When to pay

Once you pass the checks, we will send you a proforma invoice with details on how to pay.
