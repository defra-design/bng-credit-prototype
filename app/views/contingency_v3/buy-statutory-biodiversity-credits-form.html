Before you apply

A third-party supplier will carry out customer due diligence checks. These are to check you are who you say you are and assess any risks for money laundering.

You cannot use an agent or representative to act on your behalf to buy statutory biodiversity credits.

If you're buying as an individual or as an organisation, payment must be made from a bank account under the same name.

How to apply

We advise using Google Chrome to download and complete this form.
  1. Open and download the ‘Buy statutory biodiversity credits’ form. Answer the questions on the form and save it.
  2. Create a new email using your preferred email provider.
  3. Attach your completed form and your completed statutory biodiversity metric calculation.
  4. In the subject line type 'Buy statutory biodiversity credits application’.
  5. Send your email to bngcredits@naturalengland.org.uk.
Do not send your documents until we ask for them. We will not accept any handwritten forms sent in by post.

When to pay

We will contact you within 8 weeks of receiving your application to tell you if you’ve passed the checks.

We will email you with a proforma invoice if you pass the checks. By paying the proforma invoice, you accept the terms and conditions.

Cancellation of orders and refunds

You can cancel any time before paying for your credits. You will be able to get a refund if:

  • your LPA cancel your planning permission and will not reimburse you
  • you have overpaid
