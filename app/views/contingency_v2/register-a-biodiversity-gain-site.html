Register a biodiversity gain site

You can apply to register a biodiversity gain site if you're:

  • a landowner or leaseholder
  • an agent acting on their behalf

How to apply

  1. If you're an agent, you must get the person you're acting on behalf of to complete the agent authorisation form. This must be sent with your application.
  2. Download and complete the Register a biodiversity gain site form.
  3. Create a new email and attach your completed forms. In the subject line of your email, type 'Register a biodiversity gain site application'.
  4. Send your email to bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk.
! Warning These forms must be completed on your computer using a PDF editor tool. We will not be accepting any handwritten application forms.

After you've sent your forms

We will email you back within 28 days with a unique Biodiversity Net Gain (BNG) application reference number. We will also send you instructions on how to pay.
