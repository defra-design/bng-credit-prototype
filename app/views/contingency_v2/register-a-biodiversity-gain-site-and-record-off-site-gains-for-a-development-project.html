Register a biodiversity gain site and record off-site gains for a development project

You can register a biodiversity gain site, and record off-site gains for a development project at the same time, if you are:

  • a landowner or leaseholder recording off-site gains
  • an agent acting on behalf of a landowner or leaseholder
  • a developer, ecologist or other person recording off-site gains
  • an agent acting on behalf of a developer, ecologist or other person

How to apply

  1. If you're an agent, you must get the person you're acting on behalf of to complete the agent authorisation form. This must be sent with your application.
  2. Download and complete the Register a biodiversity gain site and record off-site gains for a development project.
    • create a new email
    • in subject line add 'Register a biodiversity gain site and record off-site gains for a development project'
    • attach your completed forms
    • send your email to bng.applications@naturalengland.org.uk.
! Warning These forms must be completed on your computer using a PDF editor tool. We will not be accepting any handwritten application forms.
