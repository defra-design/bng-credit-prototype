Estimate the cost of statutory biodiversity credits

Use this service to estimate the cost of statutory biodiversity credits for a development.

We need to know whether your statutory biodiversity credit type is Area, Hedgerow or Watercourse. All habitats fit into one of these groups and in each of them, there are credit tiers. There are five credit tiers in Area, A1 to A5, one in Hedgerow, H and one in Watercourse, W.

Your statutory biodiversity credit is calculated by the Metric from the information you inputted. If you want to purchase credit units go to the Manage Biodiversity Gains service.

Start now

You’ll need to have the following information to use the service:

  • the credit type, and the credit tier – Area (A1 to A5), H or W
  • the Secretary of State’s version of the Metric
