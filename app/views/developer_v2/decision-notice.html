By law, you must register your off-site gain information on Natural England's Public Biodiversity Gain Register.

The government uses the register to manage and monitor how BNG is being met across England. You need to register your information before we can grant you planning permission.

You then need to add proof of your registration to your Gain Plan that you submit to us.
